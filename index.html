Adishakti Beats is an educational app for practising South Indian Mizhavu drumming patterns, designed as a practice tool for students who have completed our eleven-day Source of Performance Energy residential workshop.
To find out about our upcoming workshops, please visit Adishakti Laboratory for Theatre Arts Research
